Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how SMPLTSK ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (smpltsk.com) and in relation to our mobile application. We believe in being transparent about our data practices.

Quick Summary: Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. Our mobile app does not use cookies - all data is stored locally on your device.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Our Use of Cookies

On Our Website (smpltsk.com)

We use minimal cookies on our website to ensure basic functionality:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function properly, such as maintaining secure areas Session only
Analytics (Optional) Help us understand how visitors use our site (only if you consent) Up to 2 years

In Our Mobile App

The SMPLTSK mobile app does not use cookies. All app data is stored locally on your device using native storage methods:

  • iOS: Uses Core Data and local file storage
  • Android: Uses Room database and internal storage
  • No web cookies are created or accessed by the app
  • No tracking cookies are used

Third-Party Services

App Store Analytics

When you download SMPLTSK from app stores, they may collect analytics:

  • Apple App Store: May use cookies for App Store analytics (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
  • Google Play Store: May use cookies for Play Store analytics (governed by Google's privacy policy)

Website Hosting

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may set technical cookies necessary for content delivery. These are governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

What We Don't Do

We want to be clear about what we DON'T do with cookies:

  • We don't use advertising cookies
  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't sell cookie data to third parties
  • We don't use cookies for user profiling
  • We don't use social media tracking cookies

Your Cookie Choices

Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies on our website:

Browser Settings: You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

Note: If you disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly.

How to Control Cookies in Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, our website may use local storage (HTML5) for:

  • Remembering your preference for cookie consent
  • Temporary storage of form data to prevent loss

You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or by clearing your browser data.

Do Not Track

Our website responds to "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser signals. When DNT is enabled, we do not load any analytics or tracking scripts.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

Cookie Policy for Specific Regions

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We request consent before setting non-essential cookies
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • We provide clear information about each cookie's purpose

California Users (CCPA)

For California residents, under the CCPA:

  • We don't sell personal information collected via cookies
  • You have the right to opt-out of any cookie usage
  • We honor "Do Not Track" signals

Australian Users

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Privacy Amendment Act 2012:

  • We minimize cookie usage to essential functions only
  • We provide clear notice about any data collection
  • You can access and correct any personal information
