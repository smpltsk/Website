üöÄ Getting Started

New to SMPLTSK? Start here to understand the basics.

What is SMPLTSK and how does it work?

SMPLTSK is visual task management designed specifically for construction and trades. Instead of writing long text descriptions or making phone calls to explain tasks, you simply:

  1. Take a photo of the job site or problem area
  2. Tap to place numbered markers directly on the photo where work is needed
  3. Add task descriptions to each marker (tap marker to add text)
  4. Send it to your team via SMS
  5. Track progress as markers turn from red (pending) to green (complete)
üí° Time Saver: Visual instructions eliminate confusion and costly miscommunication. Workers see exactly what needs doing and where - no need to call for clarification or make return trips because something wasn't clear.

Do I need any special skills or training to use SMPLTSK?

No. If you can take a photo on your phone, you can use SMPLTSK.

There's no training required, no complex setup process, and no learning curve. The app is designed to match how construction workers naturally think - visually and practically.

üí° Mental Load Reduction: SMPLTSK frees your mind by letting you quickly delegate or save tasks. Instead of trying to remember all the details, you capture them once and move on - the app remembers for you.

How long does it take to create and send a task?

Under 60 seconds for most tasks.

Here's the typical breakdown:

  • Take photo: 10 seconds
  • Place markers: 20 seconds (for 3-4 tasks)
  • Add descriptions: 20 seconds
  • Send via SMS: 5 seconds
  • Total: ~55 seconds

Compare this to traditional methods:

  • Writing detailed text instructions: 5-10 minutes
  • Phone call to explain each task: 10-15 minutes
  • Walking workers around site explaining in person: 15-30 minutes
üí° Time Saver: One minute to create a task vs. 10-15 minutes explaining it. If you create 10 tasks per day, that's 90-140 minutes saved daily. Over a month, that's 30-45 hours of your time back.

Does the person receiving tasks need to install the app?

No - and this is one of SMPLTSK's biggest advantages.

When you share a task, the recipient receives an SMS text message with a link. They simply tap the link and see the task immediately in their mobile browser - no app download required, no login needed, zero friction.

What recipients can do without installing the app:

  • View the photo with all markers and descriptions
  • Tap each marker to read task details
  • Mark tasks as complete (markers turn green)
  • View tasks on any device (phone, tablet, computer)
üí° Ease of Use: This means you can send tasks to subcontractors, suppliers, clients, or anyone without asking them to "download the app first." Zero friction = better adoption and faster results.

What makes SMPLTSK different from other task management apps?

SMPLTSK is built specifically for visual, hands-on work - not office desk jobs.

Key differences:

  • Visual-first: Photos with markers, not text-heavy task lists
  • No app required for recipients: SMS link opens in browser instantly
  • Folder/File organization: Find job photos instantly instead of scrolling through your gallery
  • Zero learning curve: If you can take a photo, you can use it
  • Built for trades: Designed by a builder who lives this problem daily
  • Local storage: Your data stays on your device, under your control
üéØ Bottom Line: Other apps try to force construction workflows into office-style task managers. SMPLTSK works the way builders and trades actually work - visually, practically, and fast.

Why isn't my camera working in SMPLTSK?

Camera permission MUST be enabled for SMPLTSK to function. Without camera access, you cannot take photos to create visual tasks.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Issue: If you see an error when trying to take a photo, or the camera button doesn't work, you likely denied camera permission when you first opened the app. This is easy to fix.

Method 1 - Fix it in SMPLTSK (Recommended):

  1. Open the SMPLTSK app
  2. Tap the Gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è (bottom icon of the three dashboard icons)
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Find the Permissions section
  5. Make sure Camera permission is enabled/allowed
  6. Continue by tapping the Camera button to take a photo

Method 2 - Fix it in iPhone Settings (iOS):

  1. Go to your phone's Settings app
  2. Scroll down and find SMPLTSK
  3. Tap SMPLTSK
  4. Tap Camera
  5. Select Allow
  6. Return to SMPLTSK and try again

Method 3 - Fix it in Android Settings:

  1. Go to your phone's Settings
  2. Tap Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap SMPLTSK
  4. Tap Permissions
  5. Find Camera and enable it
  6. Return to SMPLTSK and try again
üìå Still Not Working? Try restarting your phone after changing permissions. If the camera still doesn't work, contact [email protected] with your device model and operating system version.

  • FILES (üìÑ) = Specific trade, area, or task type within a project

‚úÖ Using the App

Creating tasks, placing markers, and tracking completion.

How do I create a task with SMPLTSK?

It's a 4-step process that takes less than 60 seconds:

  1. Take a photo of the area where work is needed (or select existing photo from gallery)
  2. Tap on the photo exactly where you want to place a marker for the first task
  3. A numbered red marker (1) appears at that spot
  4. Type a description for that marker (e.g., "Fix crack in drywall", "Replace outlet", "Paint this wall")
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 for additional tasks on the same photo (markers #2, #3, etc.)
  6. When done adding all markers, tap Send or Share
  7. Select recipients and send via SMS
üí° Ease of Use: The interface is so intuitive that most users create their first complete task within 2 minutes of opening the app for the first time - no manual, no tutorial needed.

How many markers can I add to one photo?

Unlimited. Add as many markers as you need to fully communicate the work required.

Common scenarios:

  • Simple repair: 1-2 markers
  • Defects list inspection: 5-10 markers on one photo showing multiple issues
  • Complete room renovation: 15+ markers covering walls, ceiling, floors, fixtures
  • Complex equipment: 20+ markers labeling parts, repairs, maintenance points
üí° Mental Load Reduction: Instead of trying to remember or write down "all the things wrong with this room," just tap each problem location once as you spot it. SMPLTSK remembers everything for you - you can focus on the work, not the documentation.

How do I (or my team) mark a task as complete?

One tap marks it complete - plus team members can add comments.

When a task is finished:

  1. Open the image with markers (either in SMPLTSK app or via the SMS/WhatsApp/email link)
  2. Tap the red marker for the completed task
  3. The marker instantly turns green
  4. Optional: Add a comment or note about the task (e.g., "Used copper instead of PVC as discussed")
  5. The status and any comments automatically update on the sender's phone

For the person who sent the task:

  • You get a notification when a task is marked complete (if you've enabled Notification permission)
  • Open SMPLTSK to see the green marker and read any comments
  • No need to call or text to check progress - it updates automatically
üí° Communication & Progress Tracking: Team members can add notes like "needs more materials" or "finished ahead of schedule." You see status updates and comments in real-time without interrupting your workflow or theirs.

Can I edit or delete markers after creating them?

Yes - before sending the task batch.

  • Edit description: Tap the marker ‚Üí Select "Edit" ‚Üí Change the text ‚Üí Save
  • Delete marker: Tap marker ‚Üí Select "Delete" ‚Üí Confirm
  • Move marker: Tap and hold the marker ‚Üí Drag to new position ‚Üí Release
  • Change marker number: Markers are numbered automatically; delete and recreate in desired order if needed

Important: Make all corrections before sending the task batch. Once sent, the batch cannot be edited (for security reasons - prevents accidental or intentional deletion of tasks by team members).

What if I spot more tasks after I've already sent the photo?

Create a new task batch with the additional markers.

Once a batch is sent, it cannot be edited for security reasons. However, you can:

  • Open the same photo and add new markers
  • Send the new batch to your team
  • Both the original batch and the new batch remain accessible

This keeps a complete record of all tasks and prevents accidental changes to sent batches.

Can I add notes or comments beyond the marker descriptions?

Each marker has its own description field where you can add detailed notes.

Best practices for marker descriptions:

  • Be specific: "Replace 3-gang power outlet - current one is loose" instead of just "Fix outlet"
  • Include materials: "Paint this wall - use Dulux Antique White USA" instead of just "Paint wall"
  • Add deadlines if needed: "Fix by Friday COB - client visiting Saturday"
  • Note safety concerns: "Asbestos present - call licensed removalist before touching"

There's no character limit - write as much detail as needed for each marker.

üîß Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to fix them.

The app keeps crashing or freezing. What should I do?

Try these steps in order:

  1. Close and reopen the app: Swipe up (iPhone) or tap Recent Apps button (Android) and close SMPLTSK, then reopen it
  2. Restart your phone: Power off completely, wait 10 seconds, power back on
  3. Check for app updates: Go to App Store or Google Play, search SMPLTSK, install any available updates
  4. Check phone storage: SMPLTSK stores photos locally; if your phone storage is full, the app may struggle. Free up space by deleting old photos/videos
  5. Reinstall the app: Delete SMPLTSK, restart phone, reinstall from App/Play Store (your data is stored locally so it should persist)
üìå Still Having Issues? Contact [email protected] with:
  • Your phone model and operating system version
  • When the crash occurs (specific action that triggers it)
  • Screenshots if possible

My photos aren't saving or disappearing after I take them. Why?

This is usually a storage or permission issue.

Check phone storage:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Storage
  • If you have less than 1GB free, delete old photos, videos, or apps
  • SMPLTSK needs space to store your task photos locally

Check photo permissions:

  • Settings ‚Üí SMPLTSK ‚Üí Photos/Photo Library ‚Üí Ensure it's enabled

Try taking a test photo:

  • Take a simple photo in SMPLTSK
  • Add one marker
  • Exit the app completely
  • Reopen SMPLTSK - is the photo still there?
  • If yes, the issue is resolved. If no, contact support.

I can't send SMS messages from the app. What's wrong?

Check these potential issues:

1. SMS/Messaging permission:

  • Tap Gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Permissions
  • Enable SMS or Messaging permission

2. Cellular service:

  • Ensure you have cellular signal (Wi-Fi alone isn't enough for SMS)
  • Try sending a regular text message from your phone's Messages app - if that fails, it's a carrier/signal issue, not SMPLTSK

3. SMS plan:

  • Some phone plans don't include SMS or have run out of messages
  • Contact your carrier if you suspect this is the issue

Workaround:

  • If SMS isn't working, you can copy the task link and share it via WhatsApp, email, or other messaging apps

The markers aren't appearing where I tap them. Why?

This could be a calibration or zoom issue.

Try these solutions:

  • Zoom level: If the photo is zoomed in/out, markers might not place accurately. Reset zoom to default view
  • Screen calibration: Restart the app and try again
  • Phone screen protector: Some thick or poor-quality screen protectors can affect touch accuracy
  • Tap deliberately: Single firm tap rather than light touch

If markers consistently appear offset from your tap location, please contact support with your phone model.

I'm not receiving notifications when tasks are marked complete. How do I fix this?

Enable notifications in two places:

1. In SMPLTSK:

  • Tap Gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable

2. In phone settings:

iOS:

  • Settings ‚Üí SMPLTSK ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Allow Notifications (toggle ON)
  • Choose alert style (Banners recommended)

Android:

  • Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí SMPLTSK ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable all

üîí Privacy & Data

How your data is stored and protected.

Where is my data stored? Can SMPLTSK staff see my photos and tasks?

All your photos and tasks are stored locally on your device. SMPLTSK staff cannot access, view, or read your content.

Here's how it works:

  • Photos: Stored on your phone's local storage
  • Markers and descriptions: Stored on your device
  • Task data: All local - your device only

Think of it like a filing cabinet in your office - your data lives on your device, under your physical control.

üîí Privacy Guarantee: SMPLTSK does not access, view, read, monitor, or analyze your photos, tasks, messages, or any content you create. Your work stays private.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Your data is stored locally, so deleting the app typically deletes your data.

Before deleting SMPLTSK:

  • Export or save any important photos to your photo gallery
  • Screenshot or copy any critical task descriptions
  • Note that your phone's automatic backup (iCloud/Google Drive) may have backed up SMPLTSK data

Reinstalling:

  • If your phone backs up app data, reinstalling SMPLTSK may restore your tasks
  • This depends on your phone's backup settings

Can I export my data or tasks?

You already have your data - it's all on your device.

Since everything is stored locally:

  • Photos: Already accessible through SMPLTSK app anytime
  • Individual tasks: Share or export as needed using the Share button
  • Device backup: Your phone's backup system (iCloud for iPhone, Google Drive for Android) automatically includes SMPLTSK data

There's no hidden data that SMPLTSK holds separately. Everything you create is under your direct control on your device.

Is my data secure?

Yes - because it never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

Security features:

  • Local storage: Data stays on your device, protected by your phone's security (passcode, Face ID, fingerprint)
  • No cloud storage: We don't upload your photos to our servers
  • Controlled sharing: You decide who receives tasks via SMS
  • Encrypted transmission: When you share via SMS, the link uses secure connections

Your phone's security is your data's security. Keep your phone locked and backed up.

Does SMPLTSK collect any usage data or analytics?

SMPLTSK collects minimal, anonymized usage data to improve the app.

What we collect (aggregated and anonymized):

  • App crashes or errors (to fix bugs)
  • General feature usage (which features are used most)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility)

What we DON'T collect:

  • Your photos or images
  • Your task descriptions or marker content
  • Who you send tasks to
  • Your personal or business information
  • Your location or GPS data

See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
