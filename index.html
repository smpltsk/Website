How It Works

Three Steps.
Zero Confusion.

If your subbies can open a text, they can use SMPLTSK. It's that simple.

1
📸

Mark It

Snap a photo on site. Tap to place numbered red markers exactly where the work is needed. Add a description for each one. Done in under a minute.

2
📤

Send It

Share via SMS. Your subbies get a link — they tap it, they see the job. No app download. No login. No excuses for not knowing what to do.

3
✅

Done.

Markers go green as jobs are completed. You see it happen in real time. When every marker is green, you're off-site on time.
