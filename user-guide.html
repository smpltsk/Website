User Guide

Everything you need to know to use SMPLTSK effectively

Getting Started

Installation

Download SMPLTSK from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Create your account using your email address.

Free Version: SMPLTSK is free to use with a limit of 6 tasks on 1 image. If you need more tasks or multiple images, you'll be prompted to subscribe through the App Store or Google Play Store.

Creating Your First Task

1Take a Photo

Open SMPLTSK and tap the camera button. Take a photo of the area where work needs to be done. You can also select an existing photo from your gallery.

2Mark It

Tap on the photo where the specific task needs attention. A red marker appears. You can add multiple markers to one photo if needed‚Äîeach marker can have its own task description.

3Describe It

Tap a marker to add a description. Keep it clear and specific: "Replace this cracked tile" or "Touch up paint on this corner."

4Send It

Add contacts to SMPLTSK by manually entering them or importing from your phone's contact list. Send your task batch to any contact. If they don't have the app, they'll receive a message with a link to view tasks in their web browser.

Managing Tasks

Marking Tasks Complete

When a task is finished, tap the red marker and change it to green. This provides visual confirmation that work is done. Your team and clients can see progress at a glance.

Organizing with Folders

SMPLTSK organizes your task batches into folders:

  • Sent: Task batches you've sent to others
  • Received: Task batches others have sent to you
  • Mine: Task batches you've created for yourself
  • Drafts: Unfinished task batches you haven't sent yet

Navigate between folders to find and manage your tasks efficiently.

Editing Tasks

Before sending a task, you can edit descriptions, move markers, or make any changes. However, once a task is sent, it cannot be edited. The only change that can be made is marking it complete (red to green).

If you need to communicate additional information, use the in-app messaging feature to discuss details with the recipient.

In-App Messaging

SMPLTSK includes a built-in messaging system. Tap any task to send comments or questions to the recipient. All communication stays within the app for speed and convenience‚Äîno need to switch between SMS, email, or other apps.

Best Practices

Clear Photos

Take photos in good lighting. Get close enough to show context but not so close that the location becomes unclear. Think: "Will someone else immediately understand where this is?"

Specific Descriptions

Instead of "Fix window," try "Replace cracked pane‚Äîbottom left window." The marker shows WHERE, the description explains WHAT.

One Task, One Focus

If you have multiple issues in different areas, create separate photos. This makes it easier to track completion and prevents confusion.

Regular Updates

Encourage workers to mark tasks green as soon as they're complete. This keeps everyone informed and reduces follow-up calls.

Troubleshooting

Can't see markers clearly?

Zoom in on the photo. Pinch to zoom shows more detail around each marker.

Tasks not sending?

Check your internet connection. SMPLTSK needs connectivity to share tasks. Once shared, recipients can view offline.

Need to delete something?

Delete a marker: Press and hold the marker on screen, then follow the delete prompt.

Delete an image from a task batch: If your task batch has multiple images, press and hold the selected image from the list of images at the top of the screen, then follow the delete prompt.

Delete entire task batches: Navigate to any of your folders (Sent, Received, Mine, or Drafts), press and hold the task batch you want to delete, and follow the delete prompt. This permanently removes the task batch.
